Star Car Rental in Sarajevo

Rent a car in Sarajevo with Star and enjoy a premium drive experience at competitive prices. Our highly accessible office, strategically located near both the Sarajevo City Center and Sarajevo International Airport makes it easier for you to book your favorite car model and reach your destination in no time.

Make your Booking Online and select your travel dates and the preferred car category. Car categories that you can select from include Economy, Middle Class and Premium Models, SUVs and Monovolumens. Please note that we cannot guarantee that you will receive a specific car model, but a car from your selected category.

We also offer attractive deals and extras that will enable you to customize your transportation needs.
